? Boolean Search

  • Implicit AND :
    Space means AND (e.g. 10.0.0.1 NOT AD is 10.0.0.1 AND NOT AD)
  • "Quotes" : Exact match. Use NOT "AD" to avoid matching "application-default"
  • OR : Matches either condition. Ex: 192.168.1.1 OR 10.0.0.1
  • AND : Matches all conditions. Ex: Untrust AND 192.168.1.1
  • NOT : Excludes condition. Ex: NOT 10.0.0.1
  • Supports parentheses () for precedence.
